The Coach

A Visionary in Football Development

Coach Daniel Ogunmodede Precious is the founder and head coach of IJABALL School of Thought — a revolutionary football development institution dedicated to nurturing talent through a unique blend of tactical intelligence, physical conditioning, and mental fortitude.

With over 15 years of coaching experience across multiple continents, Coach Daniel has developed a proprietary training methodology that has produced professional players now competing in top leagues worldwide. His philosophy centers on holistic development — building not just athletes, but leaders.
